Imugi The Dragon is a 2D underwater down-scrolling game where the player controls a dragon named Imugi. Game consists of two levels: Shallows and Abyss.

  • Create a fun and challenging level in UE4 with a fast paced survival gameplay.
  • Demonstrate skills in combat design and level design.
  • Create an interesting world reusing custom static meshes from another artist.
  • Build on fundamental twin-stick shooter controls by creating more abilities for the hero character to add variety to the gameplay.

Team Size:  5

Team Role:  Level Designer

Platform:  Windows PC

Game Engine: Unity

Software Used: Photoshop

Programming: C#

Player Movement:

  • Cyber Grunt is designed to be played with a controller.
  • Left thumb stick to move the character and right thumb stick to aim and shoot.
  • Running and rolling mechanics added to allow player to move away from mob of enemies.

Enemies:

  • Three kinds of enemies that each expode and cause damage to player when they get close to the player.
  • Health of each enemy depends on their sizes. Smaller enemy has less health, and bigger enemy has more health.
  • Smallest enemy is the fastest character in the game and deals least damage to player.
  • Medium sized enemy runs at the same speed as the hero character and deals mediocre damage.
  • Big enemy is very slow but deals major damage to the player.

Enemy AI:

  • Designed to chase the hero character wherever the player goes.
  • Enemies know the location of the player at all times, and they do not rely on sight to find the player.

Enemy Waves:

  • Unlimited number of waves with increasing number of enemies in subsequent waves.
  • Enemies are spawned around the map at random locations each wave.
  • The number of each type of enemy spawned at the start of every wave varies.

Point System:

  • Player earns points for each enemy killed.
  • Each kind of enemy has different point value.
  • These points are used to use turrets scattered around the level.

Turrets:

  • Friendly turrets that can be activated and upgraded with points the player earns.
  • Turrets shoot the closest enemy in turret's field of view.
  • Designed to assist the player to survive especially in later rounds.
