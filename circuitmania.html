Circuitmania is a single-player puzzle game where the player creates circuits by connecting two points on the floor. The player creates this circuit by walking on the floor and the tiles will then turn yellow beneath them to indicate that the tile is now lit. A level is complete when a yellow tile reaches the endpoint (red tile).

Team Size:  2

Role:  Level Designer

Platform:  Windows PC

Game Engine: Unity

Software Used: Photoshop

Programming: C#

Duration: 3 weeks

Responsibilities for the project:

Level Design:

  • Designed 9 puzzles to teach the player each game mechanic and test their mastery.
  • Created top-down puzzle layouts.
  • Designed the gameplay mechanics.
  • Pitched the game to the programmer.
