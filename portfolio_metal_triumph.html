Metal Triumph is a short 2D side-scrolling action game built in Unreal Engine 4 with a 720 x 480 pixel art. Player controlls a rooster and tries to venture through secret headquarters to plant high-explosive eggs to destroy the hazardous stronghold.

  • Build a short level where the player learns how to play the game through level design.
  • Reuse limited amount of obstacles and enemies in a creative way to present new challenges that come to the player in a different way each time.
  • Design the layout of a boss battle arena to test the player's mastery of each skill earned through the gameplay up to that point.
  • Place assets with unique art style to communicate the narrative through environment objects and set the mood in the level.

Team Size:  4

Team Role:  Level Designer

Platform:  Windows PC

Game Engine: Unreal Engine 4

Software Used: Photoshop

Programming: Unreal Blueprint
Visual Scripting

Player Movement:

  • WASD movement for walking and flying.
  • Rooster's energy for flying is limited, so rooster can only go up at a certain height.

Enemies:

1. Minion

  • A regular soldier with a rifle.
  • Patrols in a certain area and searches for the rooster.
  • When he sees the rooster, he stops and shoots at the rooster.
  • If he loses sight of the rooster, he returns to patrolling.

2. Drone

  • Flies and patrols in a certain area.
  • When the rooster goes under, it shoots at the rooster but does not stop moving.

3. Helicopter

  • Always chases the rooster and shoots homing missile at the rooster
  • Homing missile targets the rooster and deals a lot of damage.

4. Triumph: The Final Boss

  • Appears at the end of the level.
  • Switches between two attack depending on whether the rooster is near the boss or not.

Combat Design:

Rooster has three kinds of attacks:

  • Shout (ranged attack)
  • Egg bomb
  • Bite (melee attack)
