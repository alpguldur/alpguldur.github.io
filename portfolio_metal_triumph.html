Metal Triumph is a 2D side-scrolling action game built in Unreal Engine 4 with a 720 x 480 pixel art style. Dr. Triumph is an evil scientist who is developing dangerous nuclear arsenal in its secret fortress. You play as a rooster and set off to an adventure of venturing through a hidden headquarter to plant high-explosive eggs to destroy the hazardous stronghold.

  • Build a short level (3-5 minutes) where the player learns how to play the game through level design.
  • Reuse limited amount of obstacles and enemies in a creative way to present new challenges to the player in a different way each time.
  • Design the layout of a boss battle arena to test the player's mastery of each skill earned through the gameplay up to that point.
  • Place assets with unique art style to communicate the narrative through environment objects and set the mood in the level.

Team Size:  4

Role:  Level Designer

Platform:  Windows PC

Game Engine: Unreal Engine 4

Software Used: Photoshop

Programming: Unreal Blueprint
Visual Scripting

Duration: 3 months

Responsibilities for the project:

Level Design:

  • Level blockout by using simple geometry for designing the flow of the gameplay.
  • Placed assets for environmental storytelling and theming.
  • Collectible item placement for rewarding the player and encouraging exploration.
  • Designed environment layout for teaching mechanics and testing player's skill during the boss battle.

Combat:

  • Placed AI paths, cover objects, and enemy spawn points on the map.
  • Used limited amount of enemy types to design different and interesting combat encounters.
  • Designed pacing of combat encounters to have highs and lows in player interest curve.

Scripting:

  • Player Input (WASD movement and ranged attack)
  • AI behavior for the Minion (rifle-man) and the Helicopter.
  • Implemented a health and an energy system as core mechanics.
  • Programmed Helicopter's homing missile.
  • Particle effects for egg bomb and player being hit.
