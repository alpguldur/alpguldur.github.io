Metal Triumph is a 2D side-scrolling action game built in Unreal Engine 4 with a 720 x 480 pixel art style. Dr. Triumph is an evil scientist who is developing dangerous nuclear arsenal in its secret fortress. You play as a rooster and set off to an adventure of venturing through a hidden headquarter to plant high-explosive eggs to destroy the hazardous stronghold.

  • Build a short level (3-5 minutes) where the player learns how to play the game through level design.
  • Reuse limited amount of obstacles and enemies in a creative way to present new challenges to the player in a different way each time.
  • Design the layout of a boss battle arena to test the player's mastery of each skill earned through the gameplay up to that point.
  • Place assets with unique art style to communicate the narrative through environment objects and set the mood in the level.

Team Size:  4

Role:  Level Designer

Platform:  Windows PC

Game Engine: Unreal Engine 4

Software Used: Photoshop

Programming: Unreal Blueprint
Visual Scripting

Duration: 12 weeks

Note: Screenshots and gameplay video show the work of the whole team. My contributions to the project for game and level design are listed down below.

Player Input:

  • WASD for walking and flying.
  • Interactible objects (elevators, collectibles, bomb planting).

Combat:

  • Rooster's shout (ranged attack)
  • Throwable egg bomb
  • Rooster's bite (melee attack)

Game Balancing and Aesthetic:

  • All player input including ranged attack, flying, etc. consume energy.
  • Energy system was designed to make the Rooster constrained in the level to have a challenge game aesthetic in this unfamiliar game world.

Enemies:

1. Minion

  • A regular soldier with a rifle.
  • Patrols a certain area and looks for the rooster.
  • Designed to impose a moderate threat to the player.

2. Drone

  • Flies and patrols a certain area.
  • When the rooster goes under it, Drone starts firing.
  • Designed to teach players that they can fly and shoot at the same time.

3. Helicopter

  • Flies around the level and fires homing missiles.
  • Causes much more damage than the Minion and the Drone.
  • Designed to increase the difficulty of the level after the player mastered the base skills of the game.

4. Dr. Triumph: The Final Boss

  • The final boss of the level and the main antagonist of the game.
  • Has two combat abilities: Flying Fist (ranged attack) and Punch (melee attack).

Enemy AI:

1. Minion

  • When it spots the rooster, Minion stops and fires its rifle.
  • If it loses the sight of the rooster, Minion goes back to patrolling.

2. Drone

  • The rate of fire increases when it locks on the rooster.
  • If the rooster is no longer under it, Drone goes back to patrolling.

3. Helicopter

  • When it spots the player, Helicopter starts chasing the rooster.
  • It will not stop chasing until the player is dead to increase the pacing of the level after it is introduced.
  • Can fly horizontally and vertically.

4. Dr. Triumph

  • Switches between its two attacks depending on the rooster's proximity to the Dr. Triumph.
  • Flying Fist locks on the rooster to test player's mastery of the flying skill.
